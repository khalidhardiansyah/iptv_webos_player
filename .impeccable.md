## Design Context

### Users
Users are viewing the application from a couch using an LG TV remote control (Magic Remote or standard D-pad). They need clear spatial navigation, extremely prominent focus states, very large typography, and high contrast. The primary job is to quickly discover and stream live TV channels, movies, and TV series with minimal friction.

### Brand Personality
Bold, striking, and slightly aggressive. The UI relies on high contrast and sharp edges rather than soft, friendly aesthetics. It feels utilitarian but modern.

### Aesthetic Direction
"Editorial Brutalism". The interface uses a true black (`#050505`) and dark charcoal (`#111111`, `#222222`) background with a high-impact Electric Chartreuse (`#ccff00`) brand color for primary actions and focus rings. Elements heavily feature sharp 4px borders, hard drop shadows (`box-shadow: 4px 4px 0px rgba(0,0,0,1)`), and scaling animations on focus.

### Design Principles
1. **Unmistakable Focus Focus**: The active element must be instantly and undeniably obvious from 10 feet away.
2. **High Contrast Legibility**: Text and icons must stand out sharply against the dark background.
3. **Decisive Actions**: Interactions should feel snappy, with clear borders and hard shadows moving predictably. 
4. **Spatial Clarity**: The layout must be strictly grid-based and column-based to ensure D-pad navigation makes logical sense.
